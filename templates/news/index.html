It's like inbox zero for world news

I have two problems with news:

  1. I waste far too much time reading news I'm only barely interested in
  2. Despite this, I still never manage to stay up to date with world news

It's embarrassing when someone asks you about some major world event and you have no idea it's even taking place.

This little app is designed to help alleviate the problem. After you create an account and login you get taken to your personalised news homepage. There you will see the most significant(*) pieces of world news since you last checked. If nothing has broken into the week's top 25 news stories since your last check you will see nothing.

It's like inbox zero for your world news needs.

Feel free to check it as often as you like - it'll never distract you if nothing interesting's happening.

Yes, in future, I plan to add functionality to ping you if something seriously important is happening.
